Writing a good content for the web starts with understanding
the user. Is broadly accepted that online readers have a shorter
attention span than people reading hard-copy text.

Understanding the user

Visitors visit a site with a specific task in mind, and leave quickly if they can't it.

Visitors tend to quick scan pages and headlines, they don't read every word.

Users switch between pages extracting the most relevant information.

Most users have limited tolerance for scrolling up and down a page.
